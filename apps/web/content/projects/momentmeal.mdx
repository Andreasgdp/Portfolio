MomentMeal is a marketplace for food and recipes. We help you find and create the best recipes and meal plans for you.

## Features

- [x] Create and share your recipes with the world
- [ ] Find recipes based on your dietary preferences
- [ ] Create meal plans and shopping lists
- [ ] Reorganize meal plans based on your schedule
- [ ] Get notified when your favorite recipes are updated
- [ ] Follow your favorite chefs and get notified when they post new recipes
- [ ] Save recipes for later
- [ ] Rate and review recipes
- [ ] Search for recipes based on ingredients
- [ ] Create a marketplace to sell your recipes and meal plans
